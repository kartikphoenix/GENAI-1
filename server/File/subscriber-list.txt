Q: Is my university subscribed to TAIR?
A: Academic users in mainland China are covered by a national subscription through the National Science and Technology Library (NSTL). Academic users in Switzerland are covered by a national subscription through the Swiss Institute of Bioinformatics (SIB). If you are an academic user in China or Switzerland, you do not need to purchase an individual or lab subscription.
